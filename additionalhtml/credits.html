Textures

All textures were specialy made for this project, except the enviroment texture. Therefor, the sandstone textures by https://texturehaven.com was used. Texture Haven provides a huge variety of professional textures up to 8k resolution for FREE! (CC0-license).
The different mining materials where obtained by using Texture Haven's sandstone texture and overlaying the above mentioned storage items.
