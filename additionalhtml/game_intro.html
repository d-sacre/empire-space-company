Captain Errol to Commander Been

Commander Been, this is Captain Errol. Your Royal Highness, Queen Victoria, has commissioned me to instruct You on your duties.

Due to the rapid progress of industrialisation, vital resources are in short supply and rise dramatically in price. Our scientists need more copper to keep ahead of our competitors. Thanks to our technical advance, we can exploit mineral deposits nobody else even knows about.

Your duties are: Enlarge the great British Empire with space colonies and send back minerals to this little football called planet Earth.

Long live the Queen and the Empire!

Your task:

Produce at least 7.5 t of copper ingots or 15 t of raw copper ore (or a mixture of both) and return savely to Earth.
